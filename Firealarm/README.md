# Firealarm Tone Generator

This circuit simulates a fire alarm sound. When button SW1 is pressed, loudspeaker connected to terminal LS1 emits a tone that gradually rises in pitch and then holds steady at a constant pitch. The tone continues until the button is released. The pitch then drops again until the sound ceases completely. This process can be repeated indefinitely.

* EDA: **Kicad 6.0.11**
* PCB dimensions: **58 x 36 mm** (single sided)
